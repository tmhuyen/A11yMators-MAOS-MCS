Master Account Authority (MAA)

Is the current version of the MAA completed by the client and submitted with this summary? *
The latest MAA must be completed by the client and submitted together with the MAOS customer summary.
Are all mandatory details completed and matching eBOS? *
Company/Business legal name, address, and contact name(s)/number(s) must be completed on the MAA and match eBOS.
Is the minimum number of authorised persons met with appropriate authority? *
Ensure at least the minimum number of authorised persons (anyone/any two) has the appropriate authority as specified on the MAA.
Do all signatories have an eBOS profile set up with KYC/AML completed? *
All signatories must have eBOS profiles and completed KYC/AML. Create new profiles where required and send completed Specimen Signature Cards to the SVS team.
Have the Authorisations and Customer Declaration & Signature sections been fully completed? *
The Authorisations and Customer Declaration and Signature sections of the MAA must be completed, with the relevant tick boxes selected as applicable.
Is the MAA dated & signed by the correct authorised persons under AAC policy? *
Verify in line with AAC Policy and Corporations Act s127:
  • If the company/trustee has 2+ directors → at least 2 directors must sign.
  • If there is 1 Director and 1 Company Secretary → both must sign.
  • If Sole Director without Company Secretary → Sole Director must sign.
Has the callback process verified the signatories (esp. for digital/inserted signatures)? *
Follow the callback process to verify signatories where signatures are digitally inserted or copy-pasted.
