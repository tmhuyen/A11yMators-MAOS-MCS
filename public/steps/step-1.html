Master Customer Number (MCN)

Is the master customer KYC/AML compliant? *
All KYC/AML requirements are completed for the Master Customer with all information attached in Siebel and KYC status of Master Customer Number is up-to-date in eBOS.
Does eBOS industry match the client’s industry? *
Industry classification of MCN in eBOS must correctly reflect the client’s industry and fall under one of: External Administration, Real Estate/Settlement Agents, Legal, Strata (WA only), or Accounting.
Do company name, address and ABN/ACN match eBOS and MAA/MAOS summary? *
Company/Business name, Registered address, and ABN/ACN for the MCN must be correct, up-to-date in eBOS, and match the details provided on the MAA/MAOS customer summary.
Are banker details and BUIDs correct in eBOS? *
If the BUID falls under Small Business, contact maos.product.enquiries@nab.com.au to confirm eligibility to be onboarded to MAOS before proceeding.
Are related entities and beneficial owners up to date on the MCN? *
KYC related entities must be up to date and all beneficial owners added on the MCN in eBOS.
For Accounting industry: has a Trust Account request letter been uploaded in Siebel? *
A “Letter requesting to open a Trust Account” must be uploaded under the MCN in Siebel (applicable to clients in the Accounting industry).
